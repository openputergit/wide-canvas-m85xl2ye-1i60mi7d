About Ayurveda Essentials

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Ayurveda Essentials began as a small family shop dedicated to bringing authentic Ayurvedic remedies to the modern world. Our founder, inspired by traditional healing methods passed down through generations, created a brand that bridges ancient wisdom with contemporary wellness needs.

Today, we source the finest herbs and ingredients from sustainable farms across India and Sri Lanka, ensuring that each product maintains the purity and potency that Ayurvedic medicine demands.

Our Commitment

We test all products for purity and potency before they reach our shelves.

All our packaging is eco-friendly and our ingredients are ethically sourced.

Education
